Commands
j!help shows all the commands.
j!about sends information, credits, and shows invite link to bot.
j!play indicate a title of a song to play, for example j!play lucid dreams.
j!ping shows the current ping to the discord api.
j!clear-queue pretty self explanatory.
j!loop loops the current queue/song.
j!np or j!nowplaying sends a sexy embed of the current song playing with a progress bar and other info.
j!pause and j!resume pauses or resumes the current song.
j!debug shows how many channels are connected to Jamble.
j!queue show the queue.
j!search searches youtube and numbers the results for you to reply with.
j!shuffle shuffles through the queue instead of playing the songs from first to last.
j!skip skips the current song and proceeds to the next one in the queue.
j!stop stops the music.
j!volume change the volume for the bot..
